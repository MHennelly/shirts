background:

  • a common way of encrypting text is to use hashing, a "hash" is what comes out of hashing
  • hashes look like this
  • hashes are really, really hard to reverse - if you could do it with SHA-512 you'd get paid a lot (like a lot a lot) of money

how it works:

  • we create a hash with SHA-512 from a unique, secret passphrase
  • we print a shirt with the hash on it
  • we mint the hash, this gives it public certification so fakes are easily spotted
  • you get the t-shirt and passphrase
  • you are the only (and we mean only) person in the entire world who can hash our t-shirt

why it works:

  • $ echo -n "PASSPHRASE HERE" | sha512sum
  • ^ run that in a command line and your output will be what's printed on our shirt
  • anyone who bootlegs our shirts will never be able to guess the right passphrase
  • bootlegs can easily be spotted this way, only legit owners can run this command successfully
